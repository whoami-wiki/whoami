# Wiki Conventions

## Page Naming
- Trips: "Location (Year)" e.g. "Mexico City (2022)"
- People: "Full Name" e.g. "Raghav Rmadya"  
- Places: Just the name e.g. "Contramar"

## Required Elements
Every trip page must have:
- {{Infobox Trip}} with dates, location, participants
- Categories: [[Category:Year]], [[Category:Travel]]
- At least one image

## Citation Template
{{Cite source|type=photo|path=/archive/...|timestamp=...}}

## Gaps
Mark information gaps with:
{{Gap|What was the name of the restaurant?}}
These get surfaced to talk pages for resolution.
